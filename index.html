The Computer

This is the start of the game. As soon as you start, you are running a computer at an extremely slow 1 computation / second.

The purpose of such a computer is to find special numbers, which are numbers that give a multiplicative 1% boost to production for each one found. However, they gradually become harder to find.

Eventually, while getting money for each special number found to buy upgrades, you reach a singularity point.
